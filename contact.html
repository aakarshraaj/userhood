Frequently Asked Questions

What information should I include in my project brief?

Include your business goals, target audience, timeline, budget range, and any specific requirements or constraints. The more context you provide, the better we can tailor our proposal to your needs.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to understand your project and discuss how we can help. This helps us provide accurate timelines and pricing for your specific needs.

How quickly can you start a new project?

Our availability varies based on current projects. Typically, we can start new projects within 1-2 weeks. For urgent projects, we may be able to accommodate faster timelines.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients across different time zones and have experience collaborating remotely. We're flexible with meeting times and communication preferences.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with a 50% upfront payment and 50% upon project completion for smaller projects. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All details are clearly outlined in our proposals.
