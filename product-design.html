Our Product Design Process

1. Discovery & Research

We start by understanding your users, business goals, and market context. Through stakeholder interviews, user research, and competitive analysis, we build a solid foundation for design decisions.

2. Ideation & Wireframing

We translate insights into concepts through collaborative ideation sessions, user journey mapping, and low-fidelity wireframes that focus on structure and user flow before visual design.

3. Visual Design & Prototyping

We craft beautiful, accessible interfaces that align with your brand and delight users. High-fidelity prototypes help stakeholders visualise the final product and validate design decisions early.

4. Testing & Iteration

We validate designs through usability testing and gather feedback to refine the experience. Every design decision is backed by user insights and business objectives.

5. Handoff & Launch

We provide developers with detailed design specifications, component libraries, and ongoing support to ensure pixel-perfect implementation and smooth product launches.

6. Post-Launch Optimisation

We monitor user behaviour, gather feedback, and iterate on the design based on real-world usage data to continuously improve user experience and business outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does product design cost in Pune?

Pricing depends on scope. Typical MVP product design projects range from ₹2.5L–₹10L. Audits start ~₹75K. We share fixed or sprint-based quotes after a short discovery call.

What is the usual timeline for product design?

Most MVPs take 4–8 weeks for research, design, validation, and handoff. Complex products may take 12-16 weeks. We align timelines to your release plan and business goals.

Do you work with startups and enterprises?

Yes. We've designed for early-stage founders through enterprise teams, adapting our process and documentation depth accordingly. Every client gets the same quality, regardless of size.

What do we get at the end of the project?

Figma files, clickable prototypes, component library (if in scope), redlines/specs for engineering, research artifacts, and a style guide for consistent implementation.

Can you work remote or onsite in Pune?

Both. We collaborate async and can meet across Pune's major tech hubs when needed. Most of our process is optimised for remote work with regular check-ins and reviews.
