Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between UI and UX design?

UX (User Experience) design focuses on how a product works and feels - the overall experience, user research, and problem-solving. UI (User Interface) design focuses on how it looks - the visual design, layouts, and interactive elements. Both are essential for great products.
